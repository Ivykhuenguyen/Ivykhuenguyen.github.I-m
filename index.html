STEPS

STEP 1

Open the dishwasher,pull out the baskets

STEP 2

Put the bowls and the cups on top basket. Make sure there is enough space between the items so the water can go through. Place the items upside down

STEP 3

Close the upper basket, now start putting the plates, the pots and their lids, the pans on the bottom basket.

STEP 4

But what about the cutlery? There are two types of dishwasher, one has a small top basket that you can put them in, and the other one has a small basket inside the under brasket (where you put the plates previously), and you can put the cutlery in there

STEP 5

when you have finished putting all the things in, close the dishwaher, and put the soap cube/dishwasher detergent (there is a different place to put it in for each type) . Now adjust the washing preference (how much time, how much soap, temperature). Click start

CONGRATS, YOU HAVE DONE IT

Now, another part of using the dishwasher is how to logically organize all the things so that it fit in the dishwasher. I cannot teach you that, but overtime, you gain more experience and can use the dishwasher like a professional! Now your parents have one less thing to be dissapointed about!
