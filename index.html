HOW TO USE A DISHWASHER

this website exists because the manual is too confusing and makes your brain hurts

STEPS

STEP 1

Open the dishwasher,pull out the baskets

STEP 2

Put the bowls and the cups on top basket. Make sure there is enough space between the items so the water can go through. Place the items upside down.

STEP 3

Close the upper basket, now start putting the plates, the pots and their lids, the pans on the bottom basket.

The dishwasher cannot wash all the food pieces off the plates/bowls, so you should rinse off all the food pieces before you put it in the dishwasher!

STEP 4

But what about the cutlery? There is a small basket that is placed inside the bottom basket (where you put the plates previously), and you can put the cutlery in there.

STEP 5

when you have finished putting all the things in, close the dishwaher, and put the soap cube/dishwasher detergent (there is a different place to put it in for each type) . Now adjust the washing preference (how much time, how much soap, temperature). Click start

CONGRATS, YOU HAVE DONE IT

Now, another part of using the dishwasher is how to logically organize all the things so that it fit in the dishwasher. I cannot teach you that, but overtime, you gain more experience and can use the dishwasher like a professional! Now your parents have one less thing to be disappointed about!
